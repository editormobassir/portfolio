Frequently Asked Questions

What is your turnaround time?

Turnaround time depends on the scope and complexity of the project. For most standard editing projects, I typically deliver within 1 week. However, for larger or more detailed edits—such as documentaries or multi-camera podcasts—it may take a bit longer. I always provide a clear timeline upfront and keep you updated throughout the process.

What are your rates?

My rates vary depending on the type and length of the content, as well as any additional services you may need (such as motion graphics, color grading, or sound design). Please reach out with your project details, and I'll be happy to provide a custom quote tailored to your needs.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! I include up to 3 rounds of revisions with each project to make sure the final result aligns perfectly with your vision. Your feedback is incredibly important to me, and I'm committed to delivering work that meets (or exceeds) your expectations.
